Open Source projects Chinese companies contributed to

Posted 2019-06-25

Chinese companies have very little presence online and many people may believe that Chinese are reluctant to engage in open source projects. After a some research I found out that this is far from the truth. Reposted from my comment on Hacker News, the following is the list of open source projects which has Chinese companies as active supportors.

  • Free software Foundation patrons
  • Linux Foundation. If you frequent Kernel lists you can find many Chinese contributors (from Redhat, Huawei, etc.)
  • Eclipse Foundation
  • Apache Foundation, and many Apache projects like Apache Dubbo
  • OpenSSL exceptional support. Smartisian is a mobile phone vendor which donates their release event tickets to OpenSSL back when the HEARTBLEED security bug was a great threat.
  • Ceph Foundation
  • OpenContainers Initiative
  • CNCF members
  • OpenStack foundation
  • Xen members
  • RISC-V Foundation
  • OpenPOWER members

This list is incomplete, I hope one day there will be a Chinese company out reach more open source community like Redhat.

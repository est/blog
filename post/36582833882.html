retry alias for bash

Published: 2012-11-26
By estblog

In text.

tags: bash productivity

This is what you do when you have a shitting Internet connection:

$ vim ~/.bash_aliases

function retry(){
  false;
  while [ $? -gt 0 ];
    do $@;
  done;
}

To rape your server, just retry ssh -o ConnectTimeout=1 blah@foobar

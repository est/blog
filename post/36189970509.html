HackRF这个碉堡了

Published: Tue 20 November 2012
By estblog

In text.

tags: SDR

以前感谢一位名叫lol的网友得到了两个RTL2832U dongle开始玩rtlsdr

这玩意的参数是：

  • 64MHz - 1700 MHz (50 - 2200 MHz also possible)
  • Sample rate: 3.2 MS/s
  • Resolution: 8 bits/sample
  • receive only

关键是很便宜，大概$10左右。

今天无意中又翻到另外一个HackRF更加碉堡了。大概是$300左右价位，DARPA资助，具体参数为：

  • transmit and receive
  • operating frequency: 100 MHz to 6 GHz
  • maximum sample rate: 20 Msps
  • resolution: 8 bits
  • interface: High Speed USB
  • power supply: USB bus power
  • open source hardware and software

更喜欢HackRF的原因是其带宽。DMB-TH的带宽是4.813 Mbit/s - 32.486 Mbit/s，所以rtlsdr没法搞这个。

HackRF在github搞软件开源，同时也有硬件开源，很有意思。
